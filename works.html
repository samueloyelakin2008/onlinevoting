How It Works

Welcome to the Official Voting Platform! Our system is designed to make voting simple, secure, and transparent for every user.

To cast your vote, you select your preferred candidates and complete a secure payment of ₦100 per candidate via Paystack, a trusted payment processor. This payment confirms your vote and helps maintain the integrity of the election process.

Once your payment is verified, your vote is recorded securely and anonymously in our system. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data and ensure that each vote counts only once.

Our platform is built to be user-friendly, allowing you to participate anytime and anywhere with confidence.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact us through the provided support channels.
